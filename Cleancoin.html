Background
In 2010, 8 million metric tons of plastic was dumped into the ocean globally.
Marine debris comes from various sources. Intentional dumping and littering are one of the big causes. Indirect literring from a city thousands of kilometers away can end up at a beach near you. Rainwater moves careless waste into the ocean. Once debris gets into the ocean, it's diffic ult to locate the exact source. Beaches need constant cleanup to help releive this issue.
Image by James from Unsplash
Framing the issue
Why are clean up efforts low?
1. Lack of accessible methods to detect incoming pollution / current pollution
There is no technology to detect incoming pollution as a lot of it comes from the sea. Pattern of marine debris movement is unpredictable, and efforts to clean up feel inefficient.
2. Lack of clean-up efforts and overall lack of understanding on individual impact
Visitors and indivuals are not aware of the impact they can have if they lend a helping hand. They also lack incentive as they will only be at the beach for a few hours.
To begin to solve these problems, we explore the types of users that come to the beach.

Inspiration
Locals around beaches have been harnessing the efforts of visitors to help clean up. In addition to feeling good, they offer goods and services from their own businesses as incentives for beachgoers.

Our solution
CleanCoin
CleanCoins are collected by converting from litter picked up on the beach. They can be exchanged for goods sold by local vendors around the beach area. This idea has long existed as local vendors attempt to use their business as an initiative to help clean up the beach.
Envisioned Narrative
Using IoT for a more fun, effective beach cleanup
CleanCoin is a system that uses aerial or drone technology to capture images of debris on the beach. Images of debris are mapped onto a map, shared to the Cleancoin mobile application. Beachgoers pick up trash by following the map, and receive CleanCoins aftre the trash is inputted into smart trashcans. With Cleancoins, they can enjoy snacks, drinks, souveniers, and more from local vendors.
