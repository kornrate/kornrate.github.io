Agoda Family is a flight-hotel booking package specifically catered to families with young children.

  • Role

  • This is an individual project with small contributions with the user research and design team
  • Conducted primary and secondary user research about young families to frame design goals

  • Results

  • Designed interfaces that solve pain points for young families and fulfill design goals
  • Integrated designed interfaces into the current Agoda Flights flow
Why family flights?
Family travel has always been a big part of life in Asia.
Hotel bookings have benefitted from providing information to young families who need more support for their children. As Agoda newly ventures into becoming a flight booking agency, an opportunity for Agoda to become a one-stop site for travel emerges by combining reservations for flights and hotels for young families.
Initial Research
Along the way, I found that big competitors did not have comprehensive information for young families on both flights and hotels.
To build a rough draft of how typical users and young families complete flight and accommodation bookings, I looked into many easy-to-access pools of information: blogs, articles, airline sites, videos.
Primary Research
5 adults with young families were carefully interviewed to understand their specific concerns and experiences when traveling with a young child.
Insight 1
There are differences in concerns and priorities for long haul and short haul flights. For short haul flights: users generally prioritize cost then timing, for long haul flights: users balance between comfort, timing, and cost.
Insight 2
There is a lack of easily accessed specific information crucial to young families that help make their decision, for example: the availability and costs of bringing strollers (availability, costs), type of beds in their chosen accomodation.
Insight 3
Parents use a variety of online sources to find their information: blogs, videos, Facebook groups. Information is inconveniently accessed, and could be untrustworthy.
Insight 4
There is prevalent confusion about pricing and baggage policy for infants. Confusion leads parents out of the flight booking platform to search for information from other sources.
Scope
Young families with infants aged 0-2
Following the research, our scope is narrowed down to support young families with infants aged 0-2. Infants in this age need the most support, and young parents need all the clarity they can get before beginning their journey.
Design Goal 1
Clarify policies about infant seat pricing and baggage policy
To book a flight, baggage and seat policies are most crucial for new parents. Information will be clearly presented as parents book their flight to prevent them from leaving the platform for information elsewhere.
Design Goal 2
Surface relevant information before and after purchase
Parents are concerned throughout their journey. The more information, the more reassurance and control they have over their trip. Surfacing information at the right time help provide a sense of support for new parents.
Solution
Homepage
Catered experience
For clarity about how their infant will be charged for the flights, young families can immediately decide whether they want their infant on their lap or in their seat. Knowing early that an infant is along for the trip, we can provide helpful information in later stages as well.

Booking form
Informational consistency
We consistently provide information for young families so they are able to make their booking decision without leaving the Agoda site to find information elsewhere.
Baggage
Baggage policy confusion is a crucial pain point for traveling parents. To help ease their plans, we provide clarity about the most common confusion points about baggage.
Regulations
Regulations for infant seating are specific. We explicitly inform users of the rules for different airlines and flights.
Along the journey
Young families need special care all along their journey, including layovers. The last bit of this booking journey reassures parents that they will be prepared in every step of the way.
